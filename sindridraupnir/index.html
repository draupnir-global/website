About

The operating forge

Sindri Draupnir GmbH is the primary operating entity of the Draupnir Global group. We execute across precious metals, strategic advisory, and international trade — with the precision of Swiss governance and the reach of a global network.

Named after Sindri, the Norse dwarf-smith who forged both Draupnir and Mjolnir, our company embodies the art of transforming raw opportunity into lasting value.

Headquartered in Zug with operational reach into Latin America, Africa, and the Middle East, we bridge emerging market opportunity with first-world compliance and banking infrastructure.
