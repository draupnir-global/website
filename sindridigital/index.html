About

Digital craftsmanship

Sindri Digital GmbH is the digital assets arm of the Draupnir Global group. We operate with dedicated banking relationships, separate compliance infrastructure, and deep expertise in blockchain-native finance.

Based in Zug — the heart of Crypto Valley — we combine Swiss regulatory rigour with institutional-grade digital asset management. Our infrastructure is purpose-built for the convergence of traditional finance and decentralised technologies.

Separate entity. Separate banks. Shared precision.
