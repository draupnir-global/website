About

Built for generations

Draupnir Global GmbH is a Swiss holding company headquartered in Zug. We build and steward a portfolio of operating companies across precious metals, strategic advisory, and digital assets — guided by patience, precision, and long-term vision.

Named after Draupnir, the golden ring of Odin that multiplied eightfold with every cycle, our structure is designed for compounding value across generations.

We bring together deep industry expertise with the strategic discipline of Swiss corporate governance, operating at the intersection of traditional commerce and emerging markets.

Philosophy

Forged, not assembled

We operate at the intersection of ancient wisdom and modern enterprise. Every decision is weighed against its long-term consequence. Every partnership is chosen for endurance, not expediency.

Our companies are forged — not assembled. Built with the care of master craftsmen, designed to endure market cycles, and structured to multiply value with discipline and integrity.
